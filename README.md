# HoopScript
A minimalist basketball management simulation built for mobile. It focuses on the intersection of data-driven strategy and the "scripting" of a franchise's destiny. Built with React Native and Expo, HoopScript aims to provide a fast, snappy, and statistically accurate experience for fans of the "front office" side of the game.

## Vision
Most basketball sims focus on the action on the court. HoopScript focuses on the logic behind the wins. It’s designed for the player who spends more time in the spreadsheets and the draft room than on the fast break.

## Features (On Development)
- **Dynamic Simulation Engine** - A play-by-play engine that calculates outcomes based on multiple individual player attributes.
- **Minimalist UI** - A flat, 2D aesthetic designed for clarity and speed.
- **Roster Management** - Real-time salary cap tracking, trades, and free-agency logic.
- **Deep Scouting** - A generated "living world" with incoming rookie classes and aging veterans.
